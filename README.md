# Fixed Income Project

## Overview

This repository contains a fixed-income analysis project focused on corporate bonds, credit risk, yield analysis, duration, convexity, and investment recommendations.

The project evaluates selected corporate bonds by analyzing company background, financial structure, leverage ratios, coverage ratios, bond characteristics, yield measures, and interest rate sensitivity.

## Companies Analyzed

- The Coca-Cola Company
- Eli Lilly and Company

## Project Objectives

The main goals of this project are to:

- Analyze corporate bond characteristics
- Calculate Yield to Maturity (YTM)
- Calculate Yield to Call (YTC)
- Evaluate Yield to Worst (YTW)
- Study bond duration and modified duration
- Analyze convexity and price-yield relationships
- Assess credit risk using financial ratios
- Provide an investment recommendation

## Key Topics Covered

### 1. Company Overview

The project includes background information on the selected companies, including business operations, market position, financial performance, and industry relevance.

### 2. Bond Characteristics

The analysis reviews important bond features such as:

- Coupon rate
- Maturity date
- Coupon payment frequency
- Callable or non-callable status
- Credit ratings
- Secured or unsecured structure
- Protective covenants

### 3. Yield Analysis

The project calculates and compares:

- Current Yield
- Yield to Maturity
- Yield to Call
- Yield to Worst

These measures help determine the expected return from each bond under different assumptions.

### 4. Risk and Sensitivity Analysis

The project evaluates bond risk using:

- Duration
- Modified Duration
- Convexity
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Price-yield relationship

This helps identify how bond prices may react to changes in market interest rates.

### 5. Credit Analysis

The project uses financial ratios to evaluate the issuer’s ability to meet debt obligations.

Key ratios include:

- Debt-to-Equity Ratio
- Total Debt to Assets
- Interest Coverage Ratio
- EBITDA to Interest Expense
- Cash Flow to Debt
- Fixed Charge Coverage Ratio

## Investment Recommendation

The final recommendation is based on the company’s credit strength, bond yields, duration, convexity, and overall risk-return profile.

For Coca-Cola bonds, the recommendation is **Hold**, supported by the company’s strong credit profile, stable cash flow, investment-grade ratings, and moderate bond yields.
